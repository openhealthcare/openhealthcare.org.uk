Efficiency

By reducing duplication of work and ensuring that new information is immediately available to everyone who needs it, teams can work more efficiently and improve communication.

Accountability

The extra data captured and the ease of reporting provided by OPAL allows a much greater degree of visibility into what’s happening on wards, allowing audit trails on data that would otherwise be lost as patients are discharged.

Safety

Patient safety is of paramount importance, and with OPAL, the chances of information getting lost on bits of paper is greatly reduced.

Research

By storing information that would otherwise never have been collected, in a format that can be easily queried, OPAL opens up a new research space for academics and researchers.
